About us

Founded in Eau Claire, WI The Barnacles is a venue and performance network, booking venues for artists around the local area since 2017. Ran by Derek Dahlk, The Barnacles goal is to bring artists around the local area a sense of community and involvement in the Eau Claire music scene in an easy suportive way. The Barnacle brings connection, inspiration, and exposure to our local artists. The Barnacle is the third rebranding of this ever evolving network.
